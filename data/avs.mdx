## Introduction

Currently all of the AVS data is collected from [AVS Directory](https://github.com/Layr-Labs/eigenlayer-contracts/blob/dev/src/contracts/core/AVSDirectory.sol) and manually cleaned and verified afterwards. We also collect AVS projects from publically submitted forms.
